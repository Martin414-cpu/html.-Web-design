Chapati Recipe

Chapati is a soft,round flatbread commonly eeaten in kenya

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of wheat flour
  • 1.5 cup of hot water
  • 3 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 0.5 teaspoon of table salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough.
  3. Cover and let the dough rest for for 20 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls.
  5. Roll each ball into a flat circle.
  6. Cook on a hot pan until golden brown on both sides.
